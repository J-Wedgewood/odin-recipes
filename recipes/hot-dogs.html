air fryer hot dog

ingredients

  • hot dogs
  • hot dog buns
  1. heat air fryer to 400 degrees
  2. place buns in air fryer and cook for 2 minutes, or until slightly toasted
  3. take out buns and place hot dogs in the air fryer, continue to cok for roughly 3 minutes
  4. place hot dog inside the bun and enjoy(note: at this time you can add any condiment you would like to your hot dog)
